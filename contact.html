Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What areas do you serve?

Dalwara Real Estate serves the entire metropolitan area including Downtown, Suburbs, Coastal Regions, and surrounding communities. We have extensive knowledge of local markets and can assist with properties throughout the region.

How do I schedule a property viewing?

You can schedule a property viewing by calling our office at (123) 456-7890, using the contact form on this page, or by contacting the listing agent directly through any property listing on our website. We typically require 24-48 hours notice to schedule viewings.

What is your commission rate?

Our commission rates are competitive and vary based on the type of property, location, and services required. We offer transparent pricing and will provide a complete breakdown of all costs during our initial consultation. Contact us for a personalized quote.

Do you offer virtual property tours?

Yes, we offer virtual tours for most of our listed properties. You can view 360Â° virtual tours on our property listings page. For properties without virtual tours, we can arrange live video walkthroughs with our agents.

How long does the buying/selling process typically take?

The timeline varies depending on market conditions, property type, and individual circumstances. On average, the selling process takes 4-8 weeks from listing to closing, while buying typically takes 6-12 weeks from search to closing. We'll provide a personalized timeline during our consultation.

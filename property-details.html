Property Description

This stunning modern luxury villa offers an unparalleled living experience in the prestigious Beachfront Area. Built in 2018, this property combines contemporary architecture with high-end finishes and premium materials throughout.

The open-concept living area features floor-to-ceiling windows that flood the space with natural light and provide breathtaking ocean views. The gourmet kitchen is equipped with top-of-the-line stainless steel appliances, custom cabinetry, and a large center island perfect for entertaining.

The master suite is a true retreat with a spacious walk-in closet and a luxurious en-suite bathroom featuring a freestanding tub and rainfall shower. Three additional bedrooms provide ample space for family or guests, each with access to well-appointed bathrooms.

Outdoor living is equally impressive with a resort-style swimming pool, covered patio with outdoor kitchen, and professionally landscaped gardens. The property is located in a gated community with 24/7 security and offers direct access to private beach facilities.
